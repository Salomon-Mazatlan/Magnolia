# Surveys

Magnolia's support for survey data lets you code open-ended responses and
understand how quantitative questions were answered.

## Importing survey data

Magnolia supports `.csv` and `.xlsx` files. Survey data stays together — it
isn't broken into individual documents.

## Coding open responses

Apply codes to open-ended answers just as you would any other text.

## Quantitative overview

See an overview of your results — box plots for numerical answers, percentage
breakdowns for categorical ones — and export it as a report.
