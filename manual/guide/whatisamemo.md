# What is a memo?

A memo is a 'sticky note' you can use to write down your thoughts. 

You can attach memos to:
- The project
- Documents
- Parts of documents (such as text or parts of images)
- Analyses
- Queries
- Survey respondents
- Survey questions
- A respondent's answer to a specific question

The manner in which you create a memo changes depending on what you are attaching it to. See [Creating Memos](/guide/creatingmemos).
