# Relationship maps

Drag codes, documents, and quotes onto an interactive canvas to map the
relationships in your data.

## Building a map

1. Open a relationship map.
2. Drag codes, documents, and quotes onto the canvas.
3. Draw connections between nodes to show how they relate.

## Working with the map

- Everything stays live: double-click any node to jump straight to it.
- Attach memos to anything on the canvas.

## Exporting

Export the whole map as a clean, scalable SVG.
