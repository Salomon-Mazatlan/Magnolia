# Transcription

Magnolia's built-in transcription module lets you transcribe audio and video
files, with synced video coding so your video and transcript share one set of
codes.

> Stub page — replace with your real instructions.

## Transcribing

1. Add an audio or video file to your project.
2. Open it in the transcription view.
3. Type as you listen — a new line starts a new timestamp.

## Foot pedals

Map your chosen hotkey to a foot pedal and control playback hands-free.

## Synced video coding

Video codes are mapped to timestamps, so your video-track codes and transcript
codes are one and the same.
